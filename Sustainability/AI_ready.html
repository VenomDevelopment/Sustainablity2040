Introduction to AI Ready Business 4.0 Award

We are excited to announce the launch the 2026 edition of “AI Ready Business 4.0” Awards. As the world continues to embrace the digital transformation of industries, the manufacturing sector in Middle East is playing a pivotal role in driving innovation and efficiency through advanced technologies. THE AI Ready Business 4.0 Awards is a groundbreaking initiative that celebrates and recognizes manufacturing companies in Middle East that are leading the way in adopting Industry 4.0 practices, enhancing their operations through digitalization, automation, and smart manufacturing solutions.
